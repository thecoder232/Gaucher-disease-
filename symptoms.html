What are the symptoms? Life expectancy?

Symptoms of Gaucher disease include:

  • A low number of red blood cells (anemia)
  • Easy bruising caused, in part, by a low level of platelets
  • Bone disease (bone pain and fractures)
  • Lung problems
  • Seizures

For type one they can live a full life if they get treatment, type two is fatal within the first 2 years, type three will have a shorter life, treatment can extend life.
